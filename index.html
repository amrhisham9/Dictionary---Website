DICTIONARY

An English dictionary is the most important thing that you will need when learning English. Successful English learners use their dictionaries all the time, that’s how they learn to use new words.

At painful times, when composition is impossible and reading is not enough, grammars and dictionaries are excellent for distraction.

― Elizabeth Barrett Browning

Dictionaries are like watches; the worst is better than none, and the best cannot be expected to go quite true.

― Samuel Johnson

Dictionaries are the record of how people are already using the language, not providers of words for us to start using.

― Janet Frame
